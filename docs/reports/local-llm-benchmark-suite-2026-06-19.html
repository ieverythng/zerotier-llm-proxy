Why DFlash Was Not Selected

Lucebox documents a 22GB minimum and a 24GB reference card for its Qwen3.6 target plus draft setup; this GPU has 16,303 MiB. Its PFlash skip-park mode is for 32GB+ cards. The prior 128k launch was therefore memory-contended. The MTP model is also rejected by DFlash because its 65 layers violate the backend's full-attention interval requirement.

Compatibility

  • The IQ4 artifact is a DFlash draft, not a target model. It is valid only with --draft.
  • The MTP Q3 model is valid in llama.cpp with the dedicated draft-mtp profile.
  • LFM2 can load alongside MTP, but only 207 MiB VRAM and 305 MiB RAM remained. It was stopped before traffic.

Safety Controls

The Lucebox launch scripts now block draft-as-target errors, the known MTP incompatibility, skip-park below 32GB, and standard DFlash launch below 22GB unless explicitly marked experimental.
